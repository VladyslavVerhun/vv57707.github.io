Important elements of football training

Below there is a short list of selected elements that are very important in football development. They influence both individual skills and team performance during matches.

  • regular physical preparation and endurance
  • better passing and shooting technique
  • communication between players on the field
  • discipline during training sessions
  • quick decision making in difficult situations
